Ingredients


Wheat: A vegan substitute rich in protein, especially whole grain wheat, offering up to 20% protein per grain. It serves as a versatile ingredient in various recipes and provides sustained energy.


Dry Fruits: Almonds, dates, walnuts, and pistachios are rich in proteins and essential nutrients. They enhance the flavor and texture of the product while providing a healthy dose of protein, fiber, and essential fats.


Millets: Despite being less appetizing, millets are a crucial ingredient in the Indian diet, rich in proteins and essential nutrients. When used as a powder base, they enhance the product's nutritional value and suitability for all age groups, aiding in weight loss and providing sustained energy.


Jaggery Powder (Organic Country Sugar): A natural sweetener high in protein, fiber, and calcium. It adds sweetness to the product while contributing to weight loss and overall health due to its nutrient-rich profile.
